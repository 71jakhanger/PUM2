About the project PUM

No matter how hard you try, sooner or later you will get bored of doing push-ups, but we want to get the idea across: all people have one thing in common, each person has exactly 24 hours in a day, and how you spend them is up to you. You probably know Ronnie Coleman, Chris Bumstead or David Goggins if you came here, but the only thing that distinguishes you from them is that they work hard in their time, and you sit around. Unlike you they work when they get bored and they don't want to, so lay down on the floor and one more set

- The organizer of this event
